Our Bookkeeping & Payroll Services

Vista IFS provides accurate, audit-ready bookkeeping and payroll support for small to mid-sized businesses, clinics, ministries, and nonprofits. Below is our transparent pricing structure.

Starter – Cash Basis ($199/mo)

Entry-Level — Cash Basis

  • Up to 200 transactions per month (combined across accounts)
  • Up to 2 accounts (bank and/or credit card)
  • Monthly reconciliations
  • Basic Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet
  • Email support

Standard – Accrual ($399–$499/mo)

Accrual Accounting — Growth Stage

  • Roughly 200–700 transactions per month
  • Up to 5 accounts (banks/credit cards/merchant accounts)
  • Full accrual-basis adjustments
  • Light A/R and A/P tracking
  • Monthly financial review meeting

Advanced – Multi-State / Nonprofit ($750–$1,250/mo)

High Complexity / Multi-Entity

  • Typically 700–1,500+ transactions per month
  • Unlimited accounts within a single QuickBooks file
  • Departments, classes, projects, and job costing
  • Grant & restricted-fund tracking for nonprofits
  • Board-ready monthly reporting and cash-flow views

Payroll Add-On (billed separately)

  • $75 per payroll run + $12 per active employee, or
  • Flat-rate options available for stable teams (quoted after review)
  • Runs on your company’s own QuickBooks Payroll subscription
  • New hires receive a secure self-onboarding link (no paper packets)

Optional Compliance & Year-End Services

  • Quarterly payroll filings and reconciliations
  • Year-end forms (W-2/W-3, 1099s)
  • Multi-state payroll setup and registrations
  • Workers’ compensation reporting / integration
  • EIN / DE 542 new-hire reporting support
  • Benefits and deduction coordination (HSA/FSA, 401(k), etc.)

QuickBooks Online & Payroll Requirements

Vista IFS provides bookkeeping and payroll services on top of your own accounting software subscription. We do not resell or mark up QuickBooks; all QuickBooks subscriptions are billed directly by Intuit.

  • QuickBooks Online is required for ongoing bookkeeping services. You may sign up directly with Intuit at quickbooks.intuit.com/pricing .
  • If you choose to add Payroll administration, Intuit may also require a separate QuickBooks Payroll subscription. Those fees are billed by Intuit and are separate from VistaIFS bookkeeping and payroll administration fees.
  • Clients who want to explore bundled QuickBooks Online + Payroll options can review Intuit’s current bundle offers here: QuickBooks Online + Payroll bundles . (Offers, trials, and promotions are controlled by Intuit and may change over time.)
  • VistaIFS can assist you with choosing an appropriate QuickBooks plan, but subscriptions are always in your company’s name, and you remain responsible for all Intuit software charges.

How Payments Work

Vista IFS uses a prepaid monthly subscription model. Services are billed in advance of the month of work.

  • We invoice through QuickBooks Payments (primary) and may use Stripe as a backup option if needed.
  • After onboarding, you’ll receive a secure payment link where you can enter your card or ACH details. Vista IFS never stores your full bank or card numbers on our own servers.
  • Monthly subscriptions are set to automatic payment on your Billing Date, consistent with our Subscription Billing & Refund Policy.

Secure Onboarding & Document Upload

All onboarding documents are uploaded through our secure online portal (no emailing sensitive files). After you complete the Client Onboarding intake, you’ll receive links to download, sign, and securely upload your engagement paperwork and supporting documents.
