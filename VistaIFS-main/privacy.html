Privacy Policy

Effective: September 11, 2025 • Vista Insurance & Financial Services (“Vista IFS”)

We provide bookkeeping and payroll services across the U.S., and insurance support for California clients. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, company)
  • Messages you send through our contact forms or onboarding forms
  • Files or documents you choose to upload or email to us
  • Usage/analytics data (pages viewed, referrers, device/browser)
  • Marketing parameters (UTM tags) if present in the URL

How we use information

  • Responding to inquiries and providing services you request
  • Preparing proposals, onboarding steps, and ongoing bookkeeping/payroll services
  • Improving our website and service experience
  • Security, fraud prevention, and compliance with legal obligations

Forms, webhooks, and chat

When you submit a form on our site (for example, contact or onboarding), we may send that information to our internal systems or CRM using a secure webhook integration (such as Zapier). We also use a chat widget (Tidio) to answer questions; if enabled, the chat provider may receive your IP address and messages so we can respond.

Cookies and analytics

We use minimal cookies to remember consent and preferences, and anonymous measurement to understand site traffic. If you accept cookies on our banner, we may also enable chat and marketing tags. You can clear cookies in your browser or contact us to adjust your preferences. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

California privacy rights (CPRA)

California residents can request access to, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold, and can opt out of certain tracking. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law (for example, bookkeeping and tax records that must be kept for a set period).
