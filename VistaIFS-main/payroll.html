Managed Payroll

Payroll you can hand off with confidence

Vista IFS runs payroll on top of QuickBooks Online + QuickBooks Payroll, so your books, paychecks, and tax filings stay in one ecosystem. Intuit provides the payroll software; Vista’s Bookkeeping & Payroll Division handles the setup, processing, and compliance.

How Vista-managed payroll works

  1. Choose your QuickBooks plan. Sign up for QuickBooks Online. At checkout, you may add QuickBooks Payroll (Core, Premium, or Elite).
  2. Invite Vista as your accountant. In QuickBooks Online, go to Settings → Manage users → Accounting firms and invite Support@VistaIFS.com.
  3. Complete Vista onboarding. Use our Client Onboarding form to share company, payroll, and billing details and download your onboarding kit.
  4. We configure and run payroll. Vista IFS handles setup, tax agency registration guidance, recurring runs, and required filings. You approve hours and final payroll before each run.

Payroll add-on pricing

Payroll is added on top of one of our bookkeeping packages (Starter, Standard, or Advanced). Fees below are Vista's management fees and do not include Intuit's software subscription charges.

Vista payroll service Vista fee
Base payroll processing (per payroll run) $75 / run
Per-employee fee (W-2 or 1099 paid through payroll) $12 / active employee per run
Standard quarterly & annual payroll filings Included with active payroll service
Multi-state setup, specialty agencies, or complex corrections Scoped separately, based on complexity

Example: 1 company, 6 employees, paid bi-weekly.
Intuit bills you directly for QuickBooks Online + QuickBooks Payroll. Vista bills approximately $75 + (6 x $12) = $147 per payroll run, plus your bookkeeping package.

What Vista handles vs. what you approve

We handle

  • Payroll setup in QuickBooks Payroll
  • Employee profiles, pay types, and deductions
  • State & local tax configuration (with your registrations)
  • Direct deposit setup and test runs
  • Routine payroll processing on your schedule
  • Standard filings (941/940, state returns, W-2/W-3, 1099-NEC)
  • Syncing payroll to your general ledger

You provide & approve

  • Accurate employee and contractor details
  • Hours, salary changes, and bonuses each pay period
  • Benefit elections and garnishment orders
  • State registration numbers and tax IDs
  • Final approval before each payroll is submitted

Who this is ideal for

  • Small businesses adding payroll to existing bookkeeping
  • Clinics and nonprofits with multi-state staff
  • Organizations that want one team to manage books & payroll

QuickBooks subscriptions & billing

QuickBooks Online and QuickBooks Payroll are products of Intuit. Your software subscription is always billed directly by Intuit. Vista Insurance & Financial Services does not control Intuit’s pricing, discounts, or promotions.

When you sign up, you may see 30-day free trials or other promotions offered by Intuit. Those promotions apply to your Intuit bill only. Vista's bookkeeping and payroll management fees are separate and are billed under your Letter of Engagement.
